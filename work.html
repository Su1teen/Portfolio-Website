002 : TARS AI

There are many chatbots that perform similar functions such as Socratic from Google, QuizBot, StudyBuddy, ALEKS from Carnegie Mellon University, etc., but they are almost all narrowly focused on some disciplines and opportunities, or on the basis of any universities, where the knowledge base can be very limited and / or irrelevant. And we would like to optimize the process of self-learning for everyone.

The main goal of our project was to optimize the learning process on online platforms with the help of an assistant based on artificial intelligence GPT. We strive to make self-education as efficient and user-friendly as possible.

Project Goal.

To achieve this goal, the assistant will have the following capabilities:

  1. Create summaries of information extracted from videos, texts or websites.
  2. Providing simple interaction for obtaining information, answers to questions.
  3. The system provides an opportunity to test the acquired knowledge through tests and other assessment methods, and then gives feedback. This feedback is a basic analysis of the responses.
  4. TARS Assistant will be able to provide you with analog courses, videos and other resources to deepen and improve your knowledge in a given direction.
  5. And many other features and tools that will be added over time.

The task of the project, that is, the application itself, is for AI to be an assistant, help and improve self-learning methods, or online learning, but at the same time, it does not draw the user's attention at all and be as simple and understandable as possible in terms of UI / UX. The assistant should rather complement what already exists, and not be something isolated.

It is also possible for AI to give directions, teach, and suggest the direction and path in which the user should learn in order to prevent the development of incorrect or erroneous information by analyzing the user's responses. Our team believes that sometimes the best teacher is our own mistakes, but there shouldn't be too many of them, and our product prevents the absorption of wrong information, and finds gaps by filling it with information.
